## T5 Status

T5 criterion from IP Section 13 / kill-criteria: **>=12 of 14 proteins
confirmed with BMRB or published 15N relaxation S2 data.**

Post-remediation count: **14 / 16 active proteins** are S2-bearing (out of
the 16 effective benchmark set after BPTI + HEWL drops and 4 additions),
which clears the 12-of-14 threshold with **2 proteins of margin**
against the T5 nominal denominator. T5 status: MET.

Kill criteria: still MET even if Pin1-WW S2 is downgraded (loop-1 Fip
caveat) — margin would drop to 1 protein, still >=12. Further losses
would require re-expansion (e.g., S6 or Fyn SH3 from
`1.1-t5-margin-candidates.md`).
