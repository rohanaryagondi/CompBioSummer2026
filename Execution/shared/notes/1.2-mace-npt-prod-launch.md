## Production driver design

`output/scripts/mace_hybrid_npt_prod.py` (~1000 lines) combines three
sources, with the recipe locked verbatim:

- Recipe block from `output/scripts/npt_diagnostics/test_L_hbonds.py`
  (production-validated NPT driver), with probe-log instrumentation
  stripped and the f32 callback simplified.
- Helpers `add_protein_sentinel_bonds` and `add_protein_hbonds_constraints`
  imported (NOT modified) from
  `output/scripts/npt_diagnostics/npt_diag_common.py` (lines 500-619).
- Checkpoint/restart, walltime guard at 22.5 hr (81000 s), append-mode
  `DCDReporter` + handle-mode `StateDataReporter` with `_initializeConstants`
  monkey-patch (the v5 GB3 fix), GPU keepalive thread (5-min cadence),
  Vesin NL monkey-patch (Sub 1.1 hang-fix), `PYTHONNOUSERSITE=1` —
  salvaged from `output/scripts/mace_hybrid_npt.py`.

Critical design decisions (NEVER deviate):

- `MonteCarloBarostat(1 atm, 300 K, freq=25)` ALWAYS-ON from build time.
  Do NOT use the freq=0 → setFrequency(25) toggle from `test_L_hbonds.py`;
  test_P proved always-on works and avoids the `reinitialize` hazard with
  `loadCheckpoint`.
- Single-stage equilibration: 50 ps NPT at dt=1 fs, then production.
- Defensive assert
  `hybrid_system.getNumConstraints() == progress["n_constraints_at_checkpoint"]`
  before `loadCheckpoint` (catches stale-checkpoint mismatch; precedent
  v5 WW job 9449439).
- TARGET_NS env var: 0.05 for diagnostic probes, 5.0 for production.
- OpenCL platform only.
- Exit code 2 = walltime guard fired; same-tag resubmit resumes from
  checkpoint via `submit_mace_npt_prod.sh` wrapper.
