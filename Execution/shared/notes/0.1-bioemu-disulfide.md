# Cross-Agent Note: BioEmu Disulfide Bond Integrity Below T3

## Finding

BioEmu v1.3.1 (model bioemu-v1.1) was tested on BPTI (3 SS bonds) and HEWL
(4 SS bonds), generating 100 conformations each. Disulfide bond integrity was
measured using **CB-CB distance** because BioEmu outputs only 5 atoms per
residue (N, CA, C, CB, O) — no sidechain SG atoms are generated.

Results at **4.5 A CB-CB cutoff** (standard crystallographic threshold):

| Protein | Overall Integrity | Weakest Bond | AK3 (<80%) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------|------------|
| BPTI | 56.1% (55/98) | Cys14-Cys38: 57.1% | **TRIGGERED** |
| HEWL | 70.7% (70/99) | Cys76-Cys94: 88.9% | **TRIGGERED** |

At a more permissive **5.0 A cutoff** (accounting for BioEmu generative noise):

| Protein | Overall Integrity | AK3 (<80%) |
|---------|-------------------|------------|
| BPTI | 67.3% (66/98) | **TRIGGERED** |
| HEWL | 90.9% (90/99) | NOT triggered |

**T3 (>95% integrity) is NOT MET for either protein at any reasonable cutoff.**

**AK3 (<80% triggers drop):**
- BPTI: TRIGGERED at all cutoffs (56.1-72.4%). Must be dropped.
- HEWL: TRIGGERED at 4.5 A, NOT triggered at 5.0+ A. Cutoff decision required.

---

## Impact

- **Alpha-M:** BPTI should be dropped from the Alpha-M benchmark protein set.
  HEWL's inclusion depends on the chosen CB-CB cutoff interpretation. Dropping
  BPTI reduces the set from 14 to 13 proteins. If HEWL is also dropped, the set
  becomes 12 proteins — the T5 minimum threshold.

- **Combined paper:** The T3 threshold (>95% SS integrity) is NOT MET. This
  was defined as a quality requirement for the combined Alpha-M + Gamma paper.
  This does not kill the combined paper — it means disulfide-containing proteins
  need special treatment in the benchmark (either dropped or analyzed separately).
  The combined paper viability depends primarily on T1-T2 (MLFF quality) and T4
  (integration signal), not T3 alone.

---

## Recommended Action

1. **Drop BPTI from the benchmark.** AK3 is unambiguously triggered. The
   remaining 13 proteins still exceed T5 (>=12).

2. **Make a cutoff decision for HEWL.** Options:
   - Conservative (4.5 A): Drop HEWL too. 12 proteins remain (T5 boundary).
   - Permissive (5.0 A): Keep HEWL. 13 proteins remain with comfortable T5 margin.
   - Compromise: Keep HEWL but flag SS bonds as a known limitation in analysis.

3. **Document CB-CB proxy limitation.** BioEmu's backbone-only output means
   true SG-SG distances cannot be measured without sidechain reconstruction.
   Phase 1 should evaluate whether sidechain reconstruction (SCWRL4 or Rosetta
   packing) on BioEmu conformations improves SS distance metrics.

4. **This finding does NOT block Phase 1.** The BioEmu test is a quality
   assessment, not a gating criterion for Phase 1 launch. Phase 1 MLFF pilots
   and BioEmu production runs proceed as planned.
