### R4: Pin1 WW residue extraction — VERIFIED

### R6: T4L Mulder 2001 — VERIFIED / CAUTION

**Critical finding**: Biochemistry 40:4458 ("Flexibility and Ligand Exchange
in a Buried Cavity Mutant of T4 Lysozyme Studied by Multinuclear NMR", Mulder,
Skrynnikov, Hon, Dahlquist, Kay 2001) is the **L99A cavity mutant paper**,
NOT WT T4L. This is a reference-state mismatch with PDB 3LZM (WT).

**Impact**: Cavity-region residues (~29 residues, ~18% of T4L) will show
systematic simulated-vs-experimental S2 deviations not attributable to the
MD method. Non-cavity residues (~125 of 164, 76% of protein) remain valid
for direct comparison.

**File updated**: `manifest.json` (t4l entry) — `bmrb_reference` and `notes`
fields now flag L99A caveat.

**Phase 2 action required**: Locate WT-matched T4L S2 data (BMRB, Meiler 2001
JACS, or alternative Dahlquist-lab publication). Candidates documented in
the new note.

**New cross-agent note**: `shared/notes/1.1-t4l-citation-verification.md`.

### R7: HPr van Nuland 1995 — VERIFIED WRONG / ESCALATED

**Critical finding**: van Nuland et al. 1995 JMB 246:180 (PMID 7853396) is a
**structure-determination paper based on NOE distance restraints**. It
contains NO 15N T1/T2/NOE data and NO S2 order parameters. The manifest
citation is WRONG.

**Literature search**: No reliable alternative HPr 15N-relaxation/S2 paper
surfaced via PubMed or BMRB instant-search. HPr's S2 source appears to be
genuinely unverified.

**Impact**: HPr cannot be used for quantitative S2 comparison in Alpha-M
as currently configured.
