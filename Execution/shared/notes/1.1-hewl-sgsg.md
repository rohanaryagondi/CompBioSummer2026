# Cross-Agent Note: HEWL SG-SG Disulfide Integrity -- DROP Recommendation

## Finding

Sidechains were reconstructed on all 99 BioEmu HEWL backbone conformations using
PDBFixer (OpenMM 8.3.1). True SG-SG distances were measured for all 4 disulfide
bonds (Cys6-Cys127, Cys30-Cys115, Cys64-Cys80, Cys76-Cys94).

**Overall SG-SG integrity at 2.5 A: 40.2% -- AK3 TRIGGERED (threshold: <80%).**

| Bond | SG-SG Integrity (2.5A) | Mean Distance (A) |
|------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Cys6-Cys127 | 71.7% | 2.83 |
| Cys76-Cys94 | 46.5% | 3.34 |
| Cys64-Cys80 | 27.3% | 4.14 |
| Cys30-Cys115 | 15.2% | 3.96 |

AK3 is triggered at all tested cutoffs (2.5, 2.8, 3.0, 3.5 A). The failure is
not ambiguous. All 4 bonds fail independently.

**Comparison with Phase 0 CB-CB proxy:** The Phase 0 CB-CB measurement at 4.5 A
showed 70.7% integrity. The true SG-SG measurement at 2.5 A shows 40.2% -- worse
than the proxy suggested. CB-CB is optimistic because it ignores sidechain
orientation: CB atoms can be ~4.5 A apart while SG atoms point away from each
other and are >5 A apart.

## Impact

### Alpha-M Track

- **HEWL must be dropped from the benchmark protein set.**
- Benchmark size: 14 -> 13 (BPTI already dropped, Phase 0) -> **12** (HEWL dropped)
- T5 threshold (>=12 proteins): MET but at boundary with zero margin
- No additional proteins can be dropped without failing T5

### Combined Paper

- T3 (>95% SS integrity) was already not met in Phase 0
- This confirms HEWL's disulfide bonds are not preserved by BioEmu
- Disulfide-containing proteins need special handling in the combined analysis
- The combined paper viability depends on T1-T2 (MLFF quality) and T4 (integration
  signal), not T3 alone

### Methodological Finding

- CB-CB distance is NOT a reliable proxy for SG-SG integrity in backbone-only models
- Future BioEmu SS assessments should use sidechain reconstruction, not CB-CB proxy
- PDBFixer is a viable sidechain reconstruction tool for this purpose (~17 min for 99 conformations)

---

## Recommended Actions

1. **Drop HEWL from the Alpha-M benchmark.** The decision is unambiguous.
2. **Update the Alpha-M protein set** to 12 proteins in all downstream planning.
3. **Flag T5 boundary risk** in the Phase 1 plan. No further proteins can be dropped.
4. **Document the CB-CB vs SG-SG methodological finding** for the paper.
5. **No action needed for Delta or Gamma tracks.** HEWL is Alpha-M specific.
