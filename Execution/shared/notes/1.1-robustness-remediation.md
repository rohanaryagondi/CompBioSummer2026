### 🚨 MAJOR FINDING (2026-04-18): MACE hybrid OpenCL throughput 8.7× slower than projected

Empirical measurements across 3 proteins × 4 hr wall each:

| Protein | Measured ns/day | Subagent A projected | Slowdown |
|---------|-----------------|----------------------|----------|
| WW (534 MACE / 7,565 total) | 0.184 | 1.96 | 10.7× |
| GB3 (862 / 9,874) | 0.111 | ~0.85 | 7.7× |
| UBQ (1,231 / 17,162) | 0.074 | 0.60 | 8.1× |

**Implication:** Phase 2 IP-scope Alpha-M MLFF (9 proteins × 10-25 ns × 2 MLFFs)
is infeasible on current hardware. Effective budget need: ~410,000 GPU-hrs vs
170,000 total project budget.

**D1 gate GO verdict stands** — hybrid API works, dynamics are stable (no NaN, max-force
check passes, PE/T stable). The finding is a throughput surprise, not a correctness failure.

**D2 gate AT RISK** — criterion G1 (≥1 MLFF stable >10 ns on ≥3 Tier B proteins) would
require 135 days wall on ubiquitin alone. Unmeetable for MACE at current throughput.
SO3LR G1 still feasible via vacuum path.

**Escalation:** `shared/help-needed/sub-1.2-phase2-mlff-scope.md` with 4 options
(reduced scope / OpenMM rebuild / SO3LR-primary / implicit solvent). Recommended:
Option 1 + Option 4 diagnostic in Sub 1.2. Pending user decision.

Full analysis: `shared/notes/1.1-mace-hybrid-validation.md` §9-10.
